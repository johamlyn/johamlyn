Woodland Bird Tables

Woodland Bird Tables are a small business who make bespoke bird tables from sustainably sourced wood.

The brief on this was to make something simple that showcased the product clearly. The business was already fairly well established but felt they were missing out on some sales and reputation by not having an online presence.

My aim with this project was to keep things simple and make the process quick. Working with a company who has very little knowledge of the internet and online marketing I was really keen to show them I could take them through anything 'technical' that seemed out of reach, and that with a bit of help having a great digital profile is something accessible to everyone.

"Thank you, Jo. We wanted something simple and stylish and that exactly what you've made. the whole process from design to set up my business emails was completely painless, and surprisingly quick. Very happy." Duncan @ Woodland Bird Tables

Disco Mountain

discomountain.com is the first website I built. It is the piece I presented for my final project when studying Web Development at General Assembly.

Studying at General Assembly was fun from start to finish and I wanted to make sure my final project reflected this.

I have always had a passion for 70's disco culture and style and whilst I know that a lot (maybe all) of the design choices I made on this site do not display the subtle style and focused UX design that I now put into practice when working with clients, this was a chance to test all the skills I had learnt on the course and make something with complete freedom.

I cant imagine I will ever be commissioned to build a website for a fictitious 1970's disco night club. However, dreams do come true and it's nice to have had the practice so I'm ready if that request ever does come in.
